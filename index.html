About Bear

Howdy, the name's Bear. I'm a full-stack web developer recently graduated and earned a Certificate from the Ohio State University Digital Bootcamp. I have skill with HTML, CSS, Javascript, React.js, and more, and my background in factory and food service work gives me a well-honed talent for working efficiently with a team-first mindset. A hobbyist coder and resident tech expert since I was a child, I have a knack for teaching myself the necessary skills to solve a problem. I set up my first Apache server in my living room at the age of 13, and ever since I have wanted to make the internet a place where everyone has a home. When designing an application, user comfort and enjoyment is my top priority.

When I'm not coding, I can be found weightlifting, home brewing beers and ciders, or driving houseplants to suicide under the guise of gardening.
